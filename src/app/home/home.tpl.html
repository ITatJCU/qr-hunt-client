How do I play?

There are a {{gameCodes.length}} QR Codes hidden in plain sight around campus displays. Locate these codes and scan them using your mobile phone, which will take you to the game's website. That's all you have to do for each code - scan it.

What do I need to scan these codes?

Your mobile phone will need an app capable of reading the QR Codes for you. There are many capable apps available, however we recommend:

  • QR Droid Private for Android Devices
  • QR Code Reader by Scan for iOS Devices

Both of these apps are free.

Are there prizes?

Indeed there are! Every hour (1, 2, 3 and 4pm), a IT@JCU will give out one 16GB iPod Nano to the highest person on the leaderboard who is present (building 134).

You can also collect a nice small prize after you have scanned 20% of the codes available. Come to the IT area to collect yours!
