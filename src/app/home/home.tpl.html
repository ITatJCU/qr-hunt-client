How do I play?

There are a {{gameCodes.length}} QR Codes hidden in plain sight around campus displays. Locate these codes and scan them using your mobile phone, which will take you to the game's website. That's all you have to do for each code - scan it.

What do I need to scan these codes?

Your mobile phone will need an app capable of reading the QR Codes for you. There are many capable apps available, however we recommend these free apps:

  • QR Droid Private for Android Devices
  • QR Code Reader by Scan for iOS Devices

Are there prizes?

Indeed there are! Every hour (1, 2, 3 and 4pm), IT@JCU will give out one 16GB iPod Nano to the highest person on the leaderboard who is present in Education Central (building 134) when we announce the winner. Make sure you're there!

You can also collect a lollipop after you have scanned 20% of the codes available. Come to the IT area in Education Central (building 134) to collect yours!

Can anyone win?

JCU staff and current JCU IT students are ineligible for the major prizes, but may win lollipops for participating.
