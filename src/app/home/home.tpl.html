How do I play?

There are several QR Codes hidden in plain sight around campus displays. Locate these codes and scan them using a hand held device, after which you will be prompted to visit a website. Follow that link to collect the scan.

What do I need to scan these codes?

Your hand held device will need an application or app capable of reading the QR Codes for you. There are many capable apps available, however we recommend:

  • QR Droid Private for Android Devices
  • NeoReader for iOS Devices

Both of these apps are completely free.

Are there prizes?

Indeed there are! Every hour a representative of IT@JCU will announce the winners.
There are prizes for the top three ranked players each round.
